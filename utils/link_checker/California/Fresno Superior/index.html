If you encounter any technical issues or need assistance with the Odyssey portal, please send an e-mail to the Portal Help Desk at portal@fresno.courts.ca.gov. Please note that the Portal Help Desk cannot assist you with questions concerning your case.

Justice partners include, but are not limited to, the District Attorney's Office, Law Enforcement, Department of Social Services, and other authorized agencies. If you are not an authorized agency, please do not register -- registration from unauthorized sources will be promptly denied.
