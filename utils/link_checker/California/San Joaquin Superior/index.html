Welcome to the Public Access Portal for the Superior Court of California, County of San Joaquin. This public access portal provides online access to court records and the court calendar.

 

For Criminal case Register of Actions prior to October 19, 2015 and information not displayed in accordance with California Rule of Court 2.507, onsite viewing is available in all court locations or copies may be purchased through a records request. https://www.sjcourts.org/divisions/records-management/
