Court Calendars

** NEW Case records search portal now available! **

SEARCH COURT CALENDARS ONLINE:
 

The court is currently experiencing an issue with the Portal and the ability to conduct hearings and calendar searches. The issue has been escalated with the court's vendor. In the interim, the court has constructed this calendar page for civil and criminal hearings. The calendar page is pulling hearing information 90 days into the future for criminal matters, and 6 months into the future for civil matters. Once the hearing search function on the Portal is operational, then this temporary calendar feature will be disabled. Users may still conduct a case number search (Smart Search) on the Portal.
    

The information provided on and obtained from this site does not constitute the official record of the Court. Please read important technical information below before utilizing the calendar search. Calendars were updated on 2020-05-27 10:00:11.

  • CRIMINAL CALENDARS
  • CIVIL CALENDARS
  • *NEW* Case Records Search (CRIMINAL AND CIVIL case types)

INFORMATION AVAILABLE ON THIS SITE:
 
  1. CIVIL CALENDARS:
    • DATE RANGE: 6 months into the future.
    • UPDATE SCHEDULE: Every effort will be made to update this file every business day.
    • DATA FIELDS: Hearing Date/Time, Case Number, Caption, Judge/Department, Hearing Type, Case Type.
  2. CRIMINAL CALENDARS:
    • DATE RANGE: 45 business days.
    • UPDATE SCHEDULE: Every effort will be made to update this file every business day.
    • DATA FIELDS: Hearing Date/Time, Case Number, Defendant Name, Judge/Department, Hearing.
