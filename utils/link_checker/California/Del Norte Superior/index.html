Information Regarding Purged Records

If you cannot find the court record you are searching for, it may be because it has been purged. The El Dorado Superior Court routinely purges court records pursuant to Government Code Section 68152. You can find a copy of Government Code Section 68152 at www.leginfo.ca.gov/calaw.html
