SELF-HELP CENTER SCHEDULE DURING SHELTER IN PLACE

The Self-Help Center in the Ukiah and Fort Bragg courthouses is closed to in-person services during the Shelter in Place. Instead, we will be able to answer your questions Monday thru Thursday 8:30am-noon at 1:00-4:00pm via email at selfhelp@mendocino.courts.ca.gov or by phone at (707) 468-2020 (if busy or you reach the recording, please call back).
