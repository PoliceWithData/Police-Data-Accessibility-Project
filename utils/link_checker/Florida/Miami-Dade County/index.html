Online Services

 Our Web Applications have been updated with a fresh, clean look and responsive design. Please provide your feedback by using the HAVE FEEDBACK? button available on the right side within the application.

Please clear your cache and your cookies before accessing the applications for the first time. Also, we recommed using Google Chrome or the latest version of Microsoft Edge.

Through the Clerk of Courts' Online Services you can access a wealth of information maintained by the Clerk's Office. There are standard online services (free of charge) and premier online services (fee-based).

Commercial Data Services

For commercial customers of the data maintained by the Clerk’s Office, you may login or register for the use of Automated Programmatic Interfaces (APIs) or to download bulk data files updated on a daily, weekly or monthly basis.   These services are fee-based and require pre-registration.
