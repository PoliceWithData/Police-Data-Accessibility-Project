If you do not know your case number, use "Search for Ticket" option which allow you to search by name, complaint number, parking notice number or DR number.

If you have your case number, complaint number or parking notice number available, you may choose the option to "Pay Your Ticket".

You can also access various court forms for printing via "Court Forms" option.

Important Note:

Online payment system is NOT available between 9:00 PM and 1:00 AM Arizona Time Zone.

The on-line Disposition Report is now available to pull up and print historical case information. In the event that you have issues using the Disposition Report option, you may e-mail the Court at courtinfo@mesaaz.gov.

The availability of the Mesa Municipal Court’s case management system data and case records are subject to the Arizona Supreme Court Records Retention Schedule.
