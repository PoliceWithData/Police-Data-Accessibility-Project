Online Fines and Fees Payment

Through this site you can pay parking tickets, Court fines and fees and review summarized status of the charges contained within a case. To use this service, you must have one of the following: your court case number, complaint number, or time payment contract number. You will not be able to use this service until your ticket has been processed. Initial processing for parking violations is approximately 72 hours after the ticket was issued. Processing for all other violations may take up to 10 days. You cannot pay for defensive driving school at this site. Once you complete your transaction, you may print the receipt page for your records.
