Subscription Services

Nebraska.gov provides services that simplify how you conduct business and interact with the State of Nebraska. As a subscriber, you will have access to a wide range of online eGovernment services offered by the State. These services allow you to take full advantage of the power and convenience of the Internet to connect with Nebraska State government 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Prices listed below are on a per record basis unless otherwise noted.

  • Courts
  • Department of Motor Vehicles
  • Secretary of State
  • Other

Courts

Appellate Court Case Search (full access)Go

The SCCALES (Appellate Court) case search system provides access to cases filed in Nebraska's Supreme Court and Court of Appeals. Appellate Case number search will display the full case details including status, documents filed, etc. Filed document images are available to view or download in PDF format. Cost is $1 for each case detail view.

View Demo

 

Appellate Court Document eFiling Go Go

eFiling is the method of electronically sending filings on existing cases to the court in which you choose to file. There are no additional fees to file a case electronically, other than the normal court-assessed fees for filing. Customer accounts for eFilers must be set up on ACH billing in order to accomplish the transfer of funds due to the Courts.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Court e-Filing Request Form

View FAQs | View Demo

 

Trial Court Case Search by Judgment DateGo

The Trial Court Case Search by Judgment Date provides access to cases filed in all of Nebraska's county courts and district courts. Trial Court Case Search by Judgment Date allows you to enter general case information criteria and generate a list of cases where judgments were entered. These general search results are free of charge. If you choose to view the details of a case including status, financial activity, documents filed, etc. a $1.00 charge will be placed on your Nebraska.gov account.

View Demo

 

Trial Court Case Searches (full access) Go

Court Case Search allows you to view case details, including status, financial activity and filed documents, on both county and district court cases. Court Case search results will include the following case types; civil, criminal, juvenile, probate, small claims and traffic cases.  Cost is $1 for each case detail view. 

View FAQs | View Demo

 

Trial Court Case Searches Bulk Subscription (full access)

Court Case Searches Bulk Subscription is ideal for those who view more than 500 court cases each month. Purchasing the subscription service allows for unlimited viewing of case details (full access). This service is offered to Nebraska.gov subscribers for a $500 monthly fee. This service includes Trial court and Appellate Court Case Searches.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Court Case Searches Bulk Subscription Agreement

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Trial Court Court Record Index

The Court Record Index service offers 3 separate files, to be delivered daily, that provide case information. You may choose to receive any of the following:

  • Case Numbers that have become sealed, offered at no cost
  • New Cases, Monthly cost of $1,000
  • Statewide Case Index, Monthly cost of $1,500

Already a Subscriber? Or interested in more information including file format, case specifications, etc?

Contact Nebraska.gov at ne-support@egov.com or call 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Trial Court Document Batch eFiling

Batch filing of existing and new Contract-Unspecified Civil cases is available to attorneys in good standing with the Nebraska Bar Association. A Nebraska.gov subscription is required. There is no additional cost for this service. Nebraska.gov will provide technical instructions (including a DTD and XML file layout). Files are transferred using FTP and filings are initiated once daily.

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Trial Court Document eFiling Go

eFiling is the method of electronically sending filings on new cases and existing cases to the appropriate court in which you choose to file. New cases can be e-filed in the civil and criminal case types. Filings in existing cases can be e-filed in civil, criminal, probate, and juvenile cases.

There are no additional fees to file a case electronically, other than the normal court-assessed fees for filing. Customer accounts for eFilers must be set up on ACH billing in order to accomplish the transfer of funds due to the Courts.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Court e-Filing Request Form

Trial Court Payment Disbursements

This service allows those that are owed money on a court case to receive money electronically, within just a few days and is offered to Nebraska.gov subscribers at no cost. Those registered for this service will also receive email notification that payment was made, including the case number, party making payment, amount and description of the judgment paid.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Court Disbursement of Funds Agreement
Please provide a voided check or a Bank DDA letter to your Agreement submission

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Trial Court Return Document eFilingGo

Return eFiling is the method of electronically sending return documents on existing cases to the appropriate court in which you choose to file. Only available for Sheriff Offices and Process Servers, court approval required.

There are no additional fees to file electronically, other than the normal court-assessed fees for filing. Customer accounts for eFilers must be set up on ACH billing in order to accomplish the transfer of funds due to the Courts.

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Workers' Compensation Court Document eFiling Go

eFiling is the method of electronically sending filings on new dockets and existing dockets to the Workers' Compensation Court. There are no additional fees to file a case electronically, other than the normal court-assessed fees for filing. Customer accounts for eFilers must be set up on ACH billing in order to accomplish the transfer of funds due to the Court.

Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV)

Online Services

Driver License Record Administration & Monitoring System Go

This service allows Nebraska.gov subscribers to maintain a listing of individuals/employees online that require frequent review of driver license records. Once the driver information is entered into the system, it can be purchased and reviewed immediately with a click of a button. The entries are retained by subscription customer account and there is no additional charge to use the service. The statutory $3.00 per record fee is charged to the subscription account for each matching record.

New! Records set up in the Administration system are able to be set up to monitor changes to accidents, withdrawals, or convictions from the prior month. View Account Settings from the system for more information

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A.

 

Driver Record Searches Go

This service allows qualified business professional Nebraska.gov subscribers to access Nebraska Driver License Records online. A $3.00 fee is charged for each successful Nebraska Driver License Record search. View driver record information including status, convictions, accidents, withdrawals and permits online.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

 

Vehicle Title, Lien, Registration Searches Go

This service allows qualified business Nebraska.gov subscribers to access Nebraska Title, Lien and Registration Records online.  A $1.00 fee is charged for each successful Nebraska Title, Lien and Registration Record Search. 

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

View Demo

 

Transcript & Certified Driver Records for City & County Attorneys Go

This service allows County and City Attorney Nebraska.gov subscribers to order certified or transcript driver records. The fees are $3.00 for a Certified Record and $4.00 for Transcript record.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Certified Driver Record Request Form.

View FAQs

 

Driver Certificate of Financial Responsibility Filing – Single Submit Go

This service allows Department of Motor Vehicle approved Insurance Companies that are Nebraska.gov subscribers to submit real-time certificate of financial responsibility filings (SR22 / SR26) to the Nebraska Department of Motor Vehicles. A $.50 per single-submit record is charged to the insurance company’s subscription account.

Already a Subscriber? Send a request to ne-support@egov.com to add this service.

 

Driver Certificate of Financial Responsibility Filing – Bulk Submit

This service allows Department of Motor Vehicle approved Insurance Companies that are Nebraska.gov subscribers to bulk submit real-time certificate of financial responsibility filings (SR22 / SR26) to the Nebraska Department of Motor Vehicles via web services. This service is offered to Nebraska.gov subscribers for a $250 monthly fee, and a $.15 per record is charged to the insurance company’s subscription account.

Already a Subscriber? Send a request to ne-support@egov.com for technical documentation and any other on-boarding requirements.

 

Other

Driver License Record Point-to-Point

This service allows Nebraska.gov subscribers to receive a driver record in ASCII or fixed width text file of driver record information including status, convictions, accidents, withdrawals and permits based on search criteria. This is used for companies that want to automate a single driver record search that will remain in the same format. The statutory $3.00 per record fee is charged to the subscription account for each matching record.

Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Driver License Record Data-to-Data

This service allows Nebraska.gov subscribers to submit a listing of driver license numbers and receive a file containing driver license records in return.  Files are transferred via FTP.  The statutory $3.00 per record fee is charged to the subscription account for each matching record.  Files can be processed up to once daily.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the DMV Batch DLR Agreement.

Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Driver License Record Monitoring

This service allows Nebraska.gov subscribers to monitor Nebraska driver license records (DLR) for changes.  A listing of records are submitted by the subscriber via FTP for monitoring.  A $.06 fee is charged per record.  If changes have been made to the driver record in the monitoring period, the full record is returned and the statutory $3.00 driver record fee is charged to the subscription account.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the DLR Monitoring Agreement.

Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Vehicle Data-to-Data

This service allows Nebraska.gov subscribers to submit a listing of license plates and receive a file containing title and registration information in return.  Files are transferred via FTP.  A $1.00 fee charged to the subscription account for each matching record. Refer to the Batch VTR Process Overview document for more information.

Already a Subscriber? Complete the DMV Batch VTR Agreement.

Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Vehicle Title, Lien & Registration Special Request

Request title, lien and registration data for a specific group of records based on search criteria you provide. Files will be delivered to the e-mail address provided.

Submit the Special Request form to Nebraska.gov to submit your order. 
Cost: $50 run fee for 1st 2000 records, then $18 for each additional 1000 records.

Complete the Driver Record Search Agreement - Attachment A

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Secretary of State (SOS)

Corporate Document eDelivery Go
This service allows you to electronically submit document(s) for filing with the Secretary of State. Customer accounts must be set up on ACH billing in order to complete the transfer of funds due to the Secretary of State.

View FAQ

 

Corporate Certificates of Good Standing Go

This service allows users to request an electronic certificate of good standing for business entities registered with the Secretary of State. These certificates include a verification ID number for electronic validation. These documents are instant-access and can be viewed in a web browser or printed. $6.50

 

Corporate Record Searches - Special Requests Go

Specify criteria for a search of the Nebraska Secretary of State Corporate Database and receive a file of all matching records. Information includes: Entity type (nonprofit, LLC, etc.), company name, officer name and principle office address (no phone numbers).  Note: The total number of matches and cost is provided prior to submitting order.

 

Corporate Records Batch

Subscribers can sign up to receive official corporation record data from the Secretary of State Business office on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis.  This is a full set of data in a fixed record length format containing multiple files that would be used in external databases.  File delivery is accomplished via FTP.  $800 monthly, $500 bi-monthly, $300 weekly

Already a Subscriber? Complete the Corporate Records Batch Agreement

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

Corporate Searches and Document Images Go

Images of filed business documents are available through the online Corporate Image searches.  Examples of documents available include: tax reports, name changes, annual filings, etc.  These documents are instant-access and can be viewed in the web browser, or printed. $0.45/page

Special request searches by secured party or debtor location are also available.

View FAQs

 

UCC Image Batch

Subscribers can sign up to receive PDF images of UCC documents filed with the Nebraska Secretary of State on a monthly basis.  File delivery is accomplished via FTP.  $800 monthly

Already a Subscriber? Complete the UCC Image Batch Agreement.

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

UCC Records Batch

Subscribers can sign up to receive official UCC record data from the Secretary of State Business office on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis.  This is a full set of data in a fixed record length format containing multiple files that would be used in external databases.  File delivery is accomplished via FTP.  $800 monthly, $500 bi-monthly, $300 weekly

Already a Subscriber? Complete the UCC Records Batch Agreement

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

 

UCC/EFS Filings Go

This service allows you to electronically file UCC and EFS Originals, Assignments, Amendments, Continuations and Terminations with the Secretary of State. Subscribers must complete the UCC Electronic Filing Agreement with the Secretary of State to access this service. Filing Costs vary

View FAQs | View UCC Originals & Statutory Liens Demo | View EFS Continuations & Terminations Demo

 

UCC/EFS Searches and Document Images Go

This service provides online access to the Nebraska Secretary of State Uniform Commercial Code and Effective Financing Statement records. Subscribers can search by original document number or debtor name only. $0.00 - $4.50

Special request searches by secured party or debtor location are also available.

 

UCC/EFS Special Requests Go

Specify criteria for a search by Secured Party, or location, of the Nebraska Secretary of State UCC Database and receive a file of all matching records. Information includes: Lien number, date of filing, debtor name, and address. $2.00 per record (up to 1000 records) then a flat rate of $2000 (1,001 + records). UCC searches by Debtor Location are $15 per 1,000 records.

 

UCC XML Filing

The UCC XML Filing Service is a SOAP-based web service, which allows filers to automate the filing process using IACA standard XML and web-based technologies. This service accepts UCC originations, continuations, terminations, financing statements, and any amendment actions permitted using XML. A Nebraska.gov subscription is required. Subscribers must complete the UCC Electronic Filing Agreement with the Secretary of State to access service. Nebraska.gov will provide technical instructions. Filing costs vary.

Contact Nebraska.gov 402-471-7810 or 1-800-747-8177

Brand Committee

Brand Inspection Fees

This service allows producers to be invoiced for the monies due for brand inspections. There is no additional cost for this service.

 

Department of Health & Human Services

Central Registry Requests Go

This service allows users to request central registry checks and pay any associated fees. $2.50 Central Registry Check Fee (pending regulations); $1.00 Online Identity verification (optional service); and $1.50 processing fee

 

Health Professional License Monitoring Go

This service allows users to sign up to be notified of changes in the status of specific health licenses managed by the Department of Health and Human Services. Upcoming license expiration as well as disciplinary action email notifications are a part of the tracking system.  $.01 per license/day ($15 monthly minimum)

View Demo

 

Department of Revenue

Sales Tax Permits Go

This service allows users to download a list of all new sales tax permits issued by the Nebraska Department of Revenue.  The information becomes available each month and includes business name and address and is available in an online report listing or a tab delimited format.  $5.50/download

 

Public Service Commission

Remittance System Go

The service allows for telecommunications carriers to submit worksheets and online payments for the Nebraska Universal Service Fund (NUSF), 911 Service System Fund (911), and the Nebraska Telecommunications Relay System Fund (TRS).

 

State Patrol

Criminal History Reports Go

Nebraska Records of Arrest and Prosecution (RAP) are available for individuals that have a finger print based arrest and resulting dispositions. This service allows users to submit requests to the Nebraska State Patrol and obtain online reports. $15.50/person of interest request
