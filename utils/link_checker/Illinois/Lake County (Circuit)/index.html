Welcome to the Lake County, Illinois, Clerk of the Circuit Court's Public Access System
This site provides access to certain civil, criminal, and traffic public records of the 19th Judicial Circuit Court of Lake County, Illinois. The information displayed is an exact copy of the case information entered into the circuit court's case management system. The court record summaries viewed here are all public records under Illinois open records law.
