  • 4/9/2020

Hon. Dorothy Brown, Clerk of the Circuit Court of Cook County, IL, announced today the official launch of CCC Attorney Portal on the Clerk’s Office’s website (www.cookcountyclerkofcourt.org/attyportal), granting attorneys with active licenses in Illinois, access to, and search abilities for, criminal cases online.

Clerk Dorothy Brown Urges Limited In-Person Contact at Clerk’s Office During Coronavirus Health Concerns, Promotes Use of Remote Access Services

  • 3/13/2020

Hon. Dorothy Brown, Clerk of the Circuit Court of Cook County, IL, is urging all court users to limit in-person contact at the Clerk’s Office during the coronavirus high-risk health environment, by increasing their use of the Clerk’s Office’s various remote access services, including the Clerk’s Office mobile app. Download the informational flyer about the available ways that the public can access the Clerk’s Office and obtain services remotely.
