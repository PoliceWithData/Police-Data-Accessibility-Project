Are you needing access to the courthouse, case information and/ other documents and worry about how you will access everything without physically going into the courthouse?  Don’t let the Coronavirus take you out.  We have the help that you need with eMagnus Lite.  Through this service, you will find everything easily.  Our service will allow subscribers to view court schedules, case information, traffic, and DUI case filing and dispositions, criminal felony, and misdemeanor case filings and dispositions, all civil case filings and judgments over the internet from anywhere 24 hours a day.  For these cases, we provide the case participant information, associated attorney's information, complete court event document history, payments and resulting balance for court-ordered fines, fees, and restitution as well as criminal backgrounds and history.
